Visualizing Human Neuroimaging Data

My interests lie in brains, data and visualization! I have a Ph.D. in Neuroscience, and recently enrolled in a Masters in order to help me to combine my interests more effectively. Firstly, working with myriad investigators at University of Maryland allows me to continue building on my knowledge and experience in MRI and data analysis. Second, my Masters at MICA in Information Visualization will enable me to apply more sophisticated analysis to MR and other data (i.e EEG, MEG, verbal report), while also providing me with the skills to visualize the data in informative and engaging ways.
